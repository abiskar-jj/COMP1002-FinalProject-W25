Resurrecting Lost Classics

At VAM Classic Kicks, we don’t just restore shoes—we reconnect you with moments you thought were lost. That scuff on the toe? It might be from a 1985 prom night. The faded logo? Perhaps a little league coach wore these to every championship. Every stitch we preserve carries someone’s story, and now it’s yours to continue.

Why This Matters to You

“Shoes are time capsules,” says Vaishnav, running a gloved finger over a 1978 Adidas sole. “When you wear a revived classic, you’re not just buying footwear—you’re becoming a shared guardian of history.”

How Three Strangers United

In 2023, Lena discovered a warehouse of unsold 1987 ASICS Gel-Lyte prototypes. Omar found Ella's Reddit post about preserving 1991 Reebok Pump technology. By 2025, this trio formalized their shared obsession into VAM Classic Kicks - named for their mission of Value, Authenticity, and Memory.
